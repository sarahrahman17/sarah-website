The Stomach

Stomach is a J-shaped ,thick-walled,bag like structure.It is widest part of Alimentary canal.
It recieved food from foodpipe at one end and opens into the small intestine into the other end.



Gastric Juice contains

MUCUS

*protects inner-lining from self-digestion.


HCL

*kills bacteria and creates acidic medium.

ENZYMES

*pepsin breaks protein into aminoacid.

Functions of stomach

1.CHURNINFG-:

Thick muscular walls contracts to churn food and mix it with digestive juice.
2.Protection by mucus-:

Mucus shields the stomach lining from action of digestive enzyme and acid.

3.Hcl kills bacteria-

Hcl destroy harmful bacteria entering with food and makes the medium acidic for enzyme.

4.Protien digestion-:

Enzyme pepsin breakdown proteins into simple amino acids.

5.Partial Digestion-:

only partial digestion occurs here.complete digestion in small intestine.

6.Food storage and passage-:

Food is stores for a few minutes to hour.then leaves as a thin paste(chyme)into the small intestine.

KEY TAKEWAY

The stomach perform both mechanical and chemical digestion,but digestion here is only partial.
Most digestion and absorption is completed in small itestine.
